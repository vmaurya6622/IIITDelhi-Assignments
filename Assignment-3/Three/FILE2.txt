Mobile Phone is often also called cellular phone.. It is a device mainly used for a voice call. Presently technological advancements have made our life easy. Today, with the help of a mobile phone we can easily talk or video chat with anyone across the globe by just moving our fingers. Hence it is called a Smart Phone. Like every device, the mobile phone also has its pros and cons which we shall discuss now.
